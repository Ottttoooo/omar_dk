Karlstad, Sweeden

Omar Al Dakkak

I've always had a knack for creativity, but it wasn't until I discovered filmmaking that I truly understood its power.

Filmmaking to me is more than an art—it's a study of reality. While we all experience the same world, our perceptions color it differently, and the camera introduces yet another perspective. This idea that what we capture on film is just one interpretation among many makes each project deeply personal and incredibly revealing. It's a thrilling challenge to convey these varied realities through my lens, offering viewers a glimpse into how diverse our world can be. Each film is a journey, and I'm eager to continue exploring new creative landscapes.
